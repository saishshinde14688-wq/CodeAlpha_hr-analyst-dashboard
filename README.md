## üìà Key Dashboard Insights

The dashboard is designed to answer critical business questions:

1.  **Attrition Rate Analysis:** What is the overall attrition rate, and how does it trend across different departments and job roles?
2.  **Key Factors for Attrition:** What are the major drivers of attrition (e.g., Job Satisfaction, Income, Work-Life Balance, Overtime, Marital Status)?
3.  **Demographics & Performance:** Analysis of employee performance ratings, age distribution, and hourly/monthly income distribution.
4.  **Work Experience:** Insights into employee tenure (Years at Company, Years in Current Role) and its relation to attrition.
